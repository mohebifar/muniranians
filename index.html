The Iranians
Student Society

MUNIranians, established in 2010, is a volunteer-based independent student club at the Memorial University of Newfoundland (MUN). We made it our mission to maintain and proclaim the Iranian culture by promoting proper social mediums. Concurrently, we support our members by offering academic assistance. At MUNIranians we proudly organize social activities to unify and support the Iranian community while cultivating and celebrating Iranian culture with other citizenries.
